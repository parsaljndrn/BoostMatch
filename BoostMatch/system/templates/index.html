ABOUT BOOSTMATCH

BoostMatch is a web-based verification tool designed to help users identify misleading social media posts, particularly those containing captions and external links. With the growing volume of information shared online, users are often exposed to posts where the caption does not accurately represent the linked content. BoostMatch addresses this issue by analyzing the relationship between a post's caption and its attached link to determine whether they are aligned or potentially misleading. The system is intended to support critical information consumption by providing an accessible and easy-to-use verification platform for everyday social media users.

How does it Work?

BoostMatch examines a social media post by analyzing the relationship between the caption and the linked content.

How we Verify?

The system applies semantic analysis to understand meaning and stylometric analysis to examine writing style.

Why this Matters?

Misleading social media posts can spread rapidly. BoostMatch supports users in verifying information before sharing.
